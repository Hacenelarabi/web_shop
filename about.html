ShopCard Story

The first ShopCard was our own

"Over a decade ago, we started a store to sell T-SHIRTS online. None of the ecommerce solutions at the time gave us the control we needed to be successful—so we built our ShopCard own. Today, businesses of all sizes use ShopCard, whether they’re selling online, in retail stores, or on-the-go."

Our mission

Making commerce better for eveyone

We help people achieve independence by making it easier to start, run, and grow a business. We believe the future of commerce has more voices, not fewer, so we’re reducing the barriers to business ownership to make commerce better for everyone.

Our people

Craeting a community for impact

Shopcard has grown from 5 people in a coffee shop to over 10,000 across the globe. With millions of businesses powered by Shopcard, we care deeply about the work we do. We’re constant learners who thrive on change and seek to have an impact in everything we do.
